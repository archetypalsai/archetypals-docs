### Need a hand? No problem! You can get help in two different ways.

#### 1. Email the Mintplex Labs Team:

Send an email outlining your issue to **Ben@archetypals.ai**. Be sure to provide a clear description of the problem you're facing.

#### 2. Discord Community:

Join AI Labs Discord [Community Server](https://discord.gg/5jc5EWsv) and post your support queries on the server to get help from the community.

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
  **NOTE**

    The AI Labs Team is less active on Discord.

    For direct assistance from the team, consider sending an email to the provided address.

</Callout>
